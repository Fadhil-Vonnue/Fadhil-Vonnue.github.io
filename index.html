Wake Up Wild flavour: Kowalski! Launches OverNight Oats

Kowalski Kwality! enters a whole new category with 3 new protein-packed flavours

Park City, Wakanda , June 1, 2026 - Kowalski, the brand known for high-protein , whole grain, super sonic breakfast and snack products, today announces its latest innovation in all-new category: Overnight Oats. Available nationally in 3 falvours, Maple Saiyyan, Dark Chocolate Rasengan and Cookie Butter, the new Product line is made with 100% whole grain oats, with 50g of protein and 50 hp Chakura per serving, Its promised to give chakra and power for the whole day.

About the Product

Designed for smarter start, Kowalski Overnight Oats are a great source of fibre and are developed with less than 10g of sugar per serving, offering a delicious breakfast option without the morning hassle. Just Pour the oats into the resealable container, add preferred milk, shake or stir and let them chill overnight for a conveniant , nourishing meal that's read the following day

In support of its mission to protect wild places for future generations, Kowalski is proud to continue its partnership with 4ocean. Overnight Oats pouches will be 4ocean Plastic Neutral Certified, meaning that for every pound of plastic used in packaging, 4ocean will remove an equivalent weight of plastic from the ocean, rivers, and coastlines
